## 14-day pilot Scope

The pilot tests whether a de-identified 2D image can be turned into a useful
educational consult artifact:

1. Source image intake.
2. Image quality review and enhancement.
3. Browser-local depth build.
4. Interactive 3D educational visualization.
5. Clinician callout or annotation.
6. Consult snapshot with provenance, confidence, and limitation text.

## Do not claim

- Diagnostic accuracy.
- Disease detection.
- Treatment planning.
- FDA clearance.
- HIPAA compliance.
- Secure PHI sharing.
- PACS replacement.
- EHR integration unless the sandbox route is actually shown.
- True anatomical reconstruction from any unclear 2D image.

## Pilot Success Metrics

- Clinician understands the source and limitations.
- Patient explanation is clearer than the flat image alone.
- Snapshot artifact is useful enough to review or send internally.
- No prospect confuses the product with diagnostic AI.
- No PHI is needed for the demo.
- Objections are specific enough to drive the next product iteration.
